Lotus crisp

荷花酥

Lotus Cake is a famous traditional snack in Hangzhou, Zhejiang. It is shaped like a lotus flower, with a clear crisp layer, beautiful and moving in appearance, and crispy and sweet when eaten.

Put Chai Ko

缽仔糕

The pastry chef puts the ingredients into a bowl or a bowl of similar shape to make it. When eating, the pudding is poured out of the pudding and eaten with a bamboo stick, so it is called "bozai cake.

Lotus Starch Balls

藕粉圓子

The round and transparent lotus root starch balls are soaked in thick soup, and they are flexible and elastic when clamped on chopsticks. They are chewy in the mouth and have a lingering fragrance.

Osmanthus Cake

藕粉桂花糖糕

Lotus root starch and sweet-scented osmanthus are both famous traditional snacks in Hangzhou, Zhejiang. This is an exquisite pastry with Jiangnan flavor.

Rose Petals Pastry

鮮花玫瑰餅

Flower Cake is a kind of shortbread made with Yunnan's unique edible roses. Anyone who has eaten Flower Cake will be captivated by its crispy taste and rich aroma.

Apricot kernel Tofu

杏仁豆腐

Ice-cold, sweet and refreshing almond tofu is made by boiling sweet almond pulp, freezing and congealing, and cutting into pieces. It is named for its shape like tofu.

Tanghulu

糖葫蘆

Red hawthorn fruit or fruit fillings such as strawberries and oranges are wrapped in crystal clear syrup and strung on bamboo sticks. The sweet and sour taste is suitable for all ages.

Yolk pastry

蛋黃酥

Egg yolk cakes are made of bean paste and plump salted egg yolk wrapped in layers of puff pastry. It looks like a full moon and is most suitable for enjoying the moon

Beh Teung Guai

白糖粿

Beh Teung Guai is the iconic street food in Taiwan. It's taste like Deep-fried Mochi. The texture is crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside.

Ginger Milk Curd

薑撞奶

Ginger milk belongs to Cantonese cuisine. It is made from milk coagulated with ginger juice and sweetened. It tastes mellow, sweet and slightly spicy.

Sachima

沙琪瑪

Shaqima is a square sweet pastry of the Manchurians in the Qing Dynasty. It is golden in color, soft and sweet in taste, and has a strong taste.
